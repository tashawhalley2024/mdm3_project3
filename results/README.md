## Index Comparison Summary

The composite index (2007–2022, 13 components) shows a significant negative effect of religious courts (p = 0.002). The WBL index (2013–2022, legal rights + health) shows the same direction (β ≈ −0.005) but not significant (p ≈ 0.59). See `index_comparison.md` for the full explanation — the difference is primarily due to the shorter time window and lower within-country variation in the WBL index.
